Projects

Digital Device Image Retrieval

Find Top10 images similar to query images from gallery database. The training data has 3096 categories, with almost 70 thousand images in total. The solution contains pre-processing, utilizing SE-ResNeXt as backbone to extract feature and post processing. Got 0.70 score in the metric of 50% top1 accuracy plus 50% mAP of top10.

Semantic Segmentation for Drivable Road Detection

Build and train a Fully Convolutional Network(FCN) to label pixels corresponding to road in images

Advanced Lane Detection

Utilize OpenCV to develop a robust pipiline for highway lane detection and identification.

Quadrotor Control and State Estimation

Enable quadrotor fly through beacons using PD outer loop positon control and locolization by fusing odometry and marker measurements with kalman filter.

6 DOF Robotics RGBD Vision Arm
